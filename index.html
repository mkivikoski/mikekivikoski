Hi! I’m Mike Kivikoski

I focus on easy to use, device agnostic, accessible experiences.

With a formal background in design, and 11 years of work experience in development, I help connect the dots from Problem identification all the way through User Research, Interviews, Testing, UX Flows, Visual Design, UI development and integration. I’ve been fortunate enough to work on multi-million dollar projects, speak at conferences around the States, and seen products I’ve worked on get shipped to literally millions of users.

I’ve worked remote for the past 3 years, while being full time employed at Cantina. Prior to that I spent 5 years running my own consulting shop. Communication and transparency are two skills I’ve learned over that time, and ones that I treasure.

A few years ago, I was given the opportunity to release a video series with O’Reilly Media, “UI Development for Engineers”, that gives a quick introduction to design and front end development to developers unfamiliar with those concepts – and it is still being purchased to this day.
